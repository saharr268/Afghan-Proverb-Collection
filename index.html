Afghan Proverbs

Learn and Enjoy of knowing more about our culture and language

Afghan proverbs, like those found in Pashto, Dari, and Pashai, often reflect the country's history, culture, and values. They are not fixed, and some proverbs evolve to reflect changing times, like those mentioning modern weapons instead of older ones.


"Five fingers are brothers but are not equals"

پنج انگشت با هم برادر، ولی برابر نیستند
